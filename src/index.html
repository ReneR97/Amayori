Quick Start Guide

  1. Authenticate: Go to the Settings tab, click the "Login Automatically" button, and log into your Domestika account in the popup window. Alternatively, copy-paste your cookie values manually.
  2. Configure Download Path: Choose your desired folder where you want courses to be saved in Settings.
  3. Check Dependencies: Ensure the `N_m3u8DL-RE` engine displays as "Installed" under Settings. If not, click the install button. Make sure FFmpeg is installed on your computer (needed for processing and transcoding).
  4. Fetch Course: Go to Scrape & Download, paste the link of any course you purchased or acquired through your Plus subscription, and click "Fetch Lessons".
  5. Download: Check/uncheck the lessons you want to download, pick your subtitle languages, toggle HEVC/transcoding preferences, and click "Start Downloads".

How to get Cookies Manually

If the automatic login doesn't work for you, you can supply your credentials using cookies from your web browser:

  • Install the Cookie-Editor extension in Google Chrome or Firefox.
  • Log in to the Domestika Website.
  • Open the extension and find the cookie named _domestika_session. Copy its **Value** and paste it into the Settings input.
  • Find the cookie named _credentials_. Copy its **Value** (which is a long string of numbers/letters) and paste it into the Credentials input.
  • Click **Save Settings**.
